How to Add Van-Deventer.info Website as a Phone app
    
1. Open web browser (chrome in android – safari for iphones)

2. Open a new tab

3. Type in – www.van-deventer.info

4. Open the family website

5. In upper right hand corner of phone open 3 dots (for android)

6. Click – “add to home screen”

This will put an app on your phone to open any time without typing the
website address.
